Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common private hospital care and visitation questions.

How do I book a consultation?

Call the number at the top or use the WhatsApp chat button to request a convenient appointment time. You can also fill out our appointment form on the website.

Do you provide overnight in-patient care?

Yes. We offer in-patient care for selected medical treatments and observation with dedicated nursing support 24/7.

What are your visiting hours for patients?

Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM daily. Please call ahead to confirm visiting hours and patient availability.

What emergency services do you offer?

We provide urgent care and minor emergency support. For major emergencies, we offer referral coordination to specialized facilities. Contact us immediately at our emergency number.

Do you accept insurance and payment plans?

Yes. We accept major health insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients. Please contact our billing department for details.

Are laboratory services available?

Yes. We offer a full range of diagnostic laboratory tests. Results are typically available within 24-48 hours depending on the test type.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring valid identification, insurance card (if applicable), and any medical records from previous treatments. Arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete registration.
